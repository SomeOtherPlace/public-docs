2. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information to create and manage accounts, verify identity, provide voice and text communication features, process payments and payouts, prevent fraud and abuse, moderate content, enforce platform rules, provide customer support, improve service quality, conduct analytics, develop new features, train and improve AI or machine learning systems where permitted by law, send service notices, provide marketing communications where allowed, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Communication Data and AI Processing

Hug may process communication data, including text, voice data, audio interactions, call metadata, and related service usage information, for service operation, safety, fraud detection, content moderation, compliance monitoring, service improvement, and AI or machine learning model development where permitted by applicable law. Data used for AI model improvement may be anonymized, aggregated, or otherwise processed to reduce identifiability.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share personal information with trusted service providers that help us operate Hug, including cloud infrastructure, database, AI, authentication, payment, payout, communications, fraud prevention, analytics, customer support, and business operations providers. These providers may include Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform, Microsoft Azure, OpenAI, MongoDB Atlas, Google, Apple, Kakao, Twilio, Payoneer, MaxMind, Google Workspace, and Microsoft 365. These providers are required to process information in accordance with applicable laws and contractual obligations.

5. International Transfers

We may transfer, store, and process personal information in countries other than the user's country of residence. We take steps designed to ensure that cross-border transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including the use of appropriate contractual and technical safeguards where required.

6. Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud and abuse, maintain security, improve the service, and protect our legitimate business interests. Some records may be retained after account deletion where retention is required or permitted by law, including for tax, accounting, payment, fraud prevention, abuse monitoring, dispute resolution, legal defense, or system integrity purposes.

7. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of processing of their personal information where such rights are provided by applicable law. Requests may be limited, deferred, or denied where necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect the rights of others, prevent fraud or abuse, preserve security, or maintain service integrity.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, mobile identifiers, pixels, SDKs, and similar technologies to operate the service, authenticate users, improve performance, analyze usage, prevent fraud, measure advertising effectiveness, and provide personalized content or recommendations. Users may manage certain cookie or tracking preferences through their browser, device, or platform settings.

9. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information, including access controls, encryption where appropriate, monitoring, and security procedures. No service can guarantee complete security, and users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own account credentials and devices.

10. Children

Hug is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children where parental consent or other legal authorization is required. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can review and take appropriate action.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates may be provided through the app, website, email, or other appropriate channels. Continued use of the service after an update means the user accepts the updated policy to the extent permitted by law.

12. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact us at support@hugapp.com.
